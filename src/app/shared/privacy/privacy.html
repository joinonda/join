3. Purpose of data processing

“Join” is a demonstration project. We do not operate a commercial service and do not collect personal data for business or marketing purposes. Any data processed (such as names or email addresses entered voluntarily within the app) is used only to demonstrate the app’s functionality locally or temporarily on a demo server.

4. Categories of data processed

  • Voluntary input data such as name, email, or task information
  • Technical data such as browser type, time of access, or IP address (transmitted automatically and possibly stored temporarily in server logs)

5. Legal basis

Processing of data is based on Art. 6 (1) f GDPR (legitimate interest) for ensuring technical functionality and user interaction within the educational context. Any voluntarily entered personal data is processed based on Art. 6 (1) a GDPR (consent).

6. Data storage and deletion

Any personal data entered in Join is stored only temporarily during your active use of the application and is deleted automatically when the project or browser session ends. We do not maintain a permanent database or user accounts.

7. Data sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties. The project may be hosted on educational or personal web servers (e.g., GitHub Pages, Firebase, or Developer Akademie servers). In such cases, hosting providers may process technical log data under data processing agreements in compliance with Art. 28 GDPR.

8. Cookies and analytics

Join does not use cookies, analytics tools, or tracking technologies for advertising or profiling. Your browser may store technical session information necessary for functionality.

9. Your rights under the GDPR

  • Right to access your personal data (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR)

To exercise your rights or for any data protection concerns, please contact us via the email provided above.

10. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to secure your data against unauthorised access or loss. However, since Join is a non-commercial educational project, no continuous server-side protection or encryption of user data can be guaranteed.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when necessary to reflect technical changes or legal requirements. The current version will always be available within the Join project.
